Brief Background of CEDA-Liberia

The Center for Environment and Development in Africa (CEDA-Liberia) is a Certified Environmental Firm within the Republic of Liberia. It was established in 2008 by the late retired, Professor Samuel W.D. Wesley, Sr. Since its establishment, CEDA-Liberia has been in good standing with the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) of Liberia. Our firm has been involved in several Environmental Consultancy projects for the last seventeen (17) years, and it has prepared environmental reports for some profile National and International Companies, including projects directly funded by the World Bank (WB), African Development Bank (AfDB), European Investment Bank, etc. Our firm has also prepared Social Safeguards reports for some Agencies of the Government of Liberia, such as the Liberia Electricity Corporation (LEC), the Liberia Water and Sewer Corporation. National Bureau of Concessions (NBC), among others.


Our firm has been awarded several Certificates/Licenses to operate in Liberia based on the EPA environmental firms’ evaluation standards, as provided for by the Environmental Protection & Management Laws (EPML) of Liberia.

Our Mission

“To support sustainable development in Liberia and Africa by delivering expert environmental consulting, building local capacity, and empowering communities to protect natural resources and adapt to climate change.”

Our Vision

“A future where Liberia’s natural resources are protected, communities are climate-ready, and development is guided by sound environmental science and inclusive stewardship.”

Our Core values

1. Environmental Stewardship -We are committed to protecting Liberia’s natural resources through responsible management, conservation, and sustainable use for present and future generations.

2. Scientific Excellence - We prioritize rigorous, evidence-based approaches in all our consulting work, ensuring that our recommendations are grounded in the best available science and tailored to the unique environmental and social contexts of our clients.

3. Client-Centered Solutions - We are dedicated to understanding our clients’ needs and delivering practical, actionable solutions that support their environmental and sustainability goals while aligning with their development objectives.

4. Capacity Building - We believe in empowering local communities, institutions, and professionals through training, knowledge sharing, and collaborative partnerships to enhance environmental management and climate resilience in Liberia and across Africa.

5. Integrity and Transparency - We uphold the highest standards of honesty, accountability, and openness in all our interactions with clients, partners, and stakeholders.

6. Innovation and Impact - We are driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to delivering measurable environmental and social impact through our consulting services.
