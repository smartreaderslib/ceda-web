KEY PROJECTS IMPLEMENTED BY CEDA

All of the below reports were prepared by CEDA, and in some cases partners (JV) and submitted to the EPA for review and approval. We can say with confidence that all of our reports were reviewed and approved by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) of Liberia.

Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)

Prepared for Energy, Infrastructure, Mining, and transport projects. Founded by World Bank, African Development Bank, Liberian Government, and private investers.

Environmental Compliance Audits

These report were prepared energy, sanitation, and mining project. These projects were founded by the national and international Organizations as well as private investers.

Resettlement Action Plans (RAPs)

We have prepared a number of RAPs report for our clients on different untakings including energy, infrastructure, mining, etc. These project were founded international and national institution institutions, government of Liberia, and private individuals.

Environmental & Social Management Plans (ESMPs) - A stand-alone report

We have prepared a number of ESMPs as stand-alone report for our clients on different untakings including energy, infrastructure, mining, etc. These project were founded international and national institution institutions, government of Liberia, and private individuals.

Environmental Project Brief

We have a good history of preparing Environmental Project Briefs reports for different project that meet national and international requirement.

Water Quality Assessment

We have prepared, at least one water quality assessment report for one of our client as a stand-alone report. This report was prepared in accordance with national and international standards.
