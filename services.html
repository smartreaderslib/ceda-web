CONSULTING SERVICES WE OFFER

1. Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)

We conduct full ESIA studies compliant with the national and international safeguard requirements for development projects in Africa.

2. Environmental & Social Management Plan (ESMP)

We prepare ESMPs to ensure effective management of environmental and social impacts throughout the project lifecycle.

3. Resettlement Action Plan (RAP)

Our team can develop comprehensive Resettlement Action Plans (RAPs) to ensure proper management of affected populations during development projects.

4. Water Quality Assessment and Analysis

We conduct and prepare comprehensive water quality assessments and analyses to ensure compliance with national and international standards.

5. Environmental & Social Compliance Audits

We are a team of environmental auditors and consultants who provide compliance audits and monitoring services.

6. Emergency Preparedness & Response Planning

We conduct emergency preparedness and response planning to ensure readiness for natural disasters and other emergencies.

7. Traffic Management

We provide traffic management services to ensure safe and efficient movement of vehicles and pedestrians.

8. Occupational Health, Safety & Environment (OHSE / HSE) Plan

We prepare OHSE plans for high risk projects.
