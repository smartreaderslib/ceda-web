Our Technical Experts

Core Technical Experts

These roles directly ensure technical quality and compliance


1. Lead Environmental Consultant / Principal Environmental Specialist

Overall technical leadership, quality assurance, and client oversight.


2. Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) Specialist

Lead ESIA studies, ensuring compliance with EPA and international safeguards.


3. Environmental Safeguards & Compliance Specialist

Ensure compliance with environmental and social safeguards, coordinate with regulatory bodies, and manage compliance reporting.


4. Climate Change & Resilience Specialist

Provide expertise on climate change mitigation and adaptation strategies, integrating climate considerations into project design and implementation.


5. Environmental Monitoring & Audit Specialist

Design and implement environmental monitoring programs, conduct environmental audits, and ensure ongoing compliance with environmental standards.

Natural Resource & Sector Experts


These roles provide sector-specific and natural resource expertise

1. Natural Resource Management Specialist

Provides expertise in forestry, biodiversity, land use, conservation planning.


2. Biodiversity & Ecosystem Services Specialist

Provides expertise in habitat assessments, species protection, offsets, ecosystem valuation.


3. Water, Sanitation & Environmental Health (WASH/EH) Specialist

Provides expertise in water quality, sanitation, environmental health risk assessments.

Social & Community Experts

These roles are critical for inclusive stewardship and project acceptance.


1. Social Development & Community Engagement Specialist

Provides expertise in Stakeholder consultations, social risk analysis, grievance mechanisms.


2. Resettlement & Livelihoods Specialist

Provides expertise in resettlement planning, livelihood restoration, and social safeguards.


3. Gender & Social Inclusion Specialist

Provides expertise in gender mainstreaming, social inclusion, and ensuring equitable access to project benefits.

Data, Planning & Technical Support Experts

These roles provide essential data analysis, planning, and technical support.


1. GIS & Environmental Data Analyst

Provides expertise in spatial data analysis, mapping, and remote sensing applications for environmental monitoring and management.


2. Environmental Research & Policy Analyst

Provides expertise in environmental research, policy analysis, and evidence-based decision-making.


3. Project & Program Management Specialist

Provides expertise in project planning, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation.

Health, Safety & Risk Experts

This roles provides expertise in health, safety, and risk management for environmental projects.


1. Occupational Health, Safety & Environment (OHSE/HSE) Specialist

Provides expertise in occupational health, safety, and environmental management for project implementation.

Leadership & Quality Control

These roles protect CEDA’s reputation.


1. Quality Assurance & Technical Review Expert

provides independent review of reports before client submission.


2. Project Manager – Environmental Services

Prepares schedules, budgets, coordination, and client communication.
